WEEK CHALLENGE

With this challenge you'll be able to save 1378 CHF. As you know, each year has a total of 52 weeks. Each week is represented by its number in this table, and it's the amount you'll have to save. Each week you have to choose a number and save that amount. You can start the year saving 1 CHF/52 CHF and continue from there in strict ascending/descending order, or you can choose the number depending on how much money you have that week.
